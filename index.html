About us

Midpoint Capital Limited is a registered company in Nigeria with RC 746044. We are a dealing member of the Nigerian Stock Exchange, offering a wide range of financial services to our clients. Our portfolio management services are designed to help our clients achieve their investment goals by carefully selecting and managing a diverse range of assets. Our stock broking services enable our clients to buy and sell securities on the Nigerian Stock Exchange with ease and efficiency. In addition to portfolio management and stock broking, we offer a range of other financial services, including securities trading and financial advisory. Our team of experienced professionals is trained in diverse sectors of the Finance Industry and has several years of experience in the field. We are dedicated to providing our clients with quality service, and we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence. Our board and management team is comprised of highly skilled professionals who are experts in their respective fields. Our staff is well-qualified and committed to providing our clients with the highest level of service possible. We believe that our expertise, dedication, and commitment to excellence set us apart from our competitors and enable us to provide our clients with the best possible financial solutions.
